Unity3D & accelerometer

Stisa, 08 July 2014

You know that feeling when you spend ours thinking about an issue, and you finally understand something very simple was missing the whole time? Well, I know that feeling very well. accel = Mathf.Round(Mathf.Sqrt( (Input.acceleration.x *Input.acceleration.x) + (Input.acceleration.z * Input.acceleration.z) + (Input.acceleration.y*Input.acceleration.y) )); Outputted 0. Zero. Always, even when shaking the phone. And then I realized Input.acceleration gives a value between 0 and 1, and then rounding down that number always ended up to zero.
